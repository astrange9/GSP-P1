Camp Kitchen and Group Camping

Golden Sheaf Park can be the center of your event. The Park has two Camp Kitchens with a place to cook and store your food for a hunger group event. Each facility has power for small appliances, Fridge and freezer and sinks for clean up.
Each area can also be home for the weekend or a couple of days
Up to 20 RV Units can camp close to the Camp Kitchen
